What is FAIR and why FAIRify the data

Findability, Accessibility, Interoperability, and Reuse of digital assets. The principles emphasise machine-actionability (i.e., the capacity of computational systems to find, access, interoperate, and reuse data with none or minimal human intervention) because humans increasingly rely on computational support to deal with data as a result of the increase in volume, complexity, and creation speed of data.

What does the FAIR wizard do?

To provide guidance on FAIRification of projects and datasets, and make FAIRplus project outcomes more accessible and reusable for external academic projects and pharmaceutical partners, we propose the creation of a FAIRplus FAIRification wizard. This web-based service will support FAIRification workflow design by data owners, data stewards and other key stakeholders, exploiting FAIRplus FAIRification solutions by aligning user needs and project outputs such as FAIR assessments and cookbook recipes. The wizard will consist of two parts: A self-assessment tool to evaluate the FAIR maturity level of the userâ€™s project, and identify competency questions and user requirements for FAIRification A FAIRification solution knowledgebase with all FAIRplus recipes, examples, indicators, and processes The wizard will map the results of the self-assessment to existing solutions, and return a personalized FAIRification workflow to the user. The personalized workflow will recommend FAIRification processes and recipes, estimates the costs and outcomes of the FAIRification work, and provides dataset FAIRfication examples.
