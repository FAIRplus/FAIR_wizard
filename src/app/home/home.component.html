FAIR principles serve to guide data producers and data publishers to improve their data management practices and improve the reuse of research data1. FAIRification is the process of improving the FAIR level of datasets, projects or related infrastructure.
The FAIR wizard utilizes FAIRification resources developed by the FAIRplus project and other platforms, suggests FAIRification materials based on the FAIRification requirements, and designs FAIRification solutions for data owners, data stewards, and other people involved in FAIRification.
The wizard is in early-stage development. Major updates are expected. Feedback is welcome. To provide feedback on general use cases and FAIRification solutions, please fill out this form. Feedback is also welcome through Github issues.
